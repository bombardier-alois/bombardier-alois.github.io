WASABI

Transmedia mobile app

The project type : school
My role : designer & ergonomist
Team : Chloé Luminet graphic designer, Quentin Hocdé project manager, Thomas Hiron developer
Duration : 6 months
Date : 2015

Spicing up everyday life, this is the promise Wasabi made. This innovation enables close relations to live the unexpected. It is a transmedia immersive experience with fun and unusual ambiance. The user receives differents items, personalized gifts, and is invited to take part to new activities, at unexpected places on several devices. The aim is to cheer up the recipient and to make him feel special with the help of the accomplice : the one who buy the service.
