EURONEWS NEW DESIGN

Website

The project type : professional
My role : UI & UX designer
Duration : 7 months
Date : October 2018 tò April 2019

As UX / UI designer at Euronews, I design the new look and feel of the website. Our main objectives were :

- Change for a more clear and readable website, highlight the content.

- Optimize recirculation , navigation to find infos

- Set up a new flexible system, that we can change depend of the currents events

- Optimize advertising

-> See the website : euronews.com

Search & analysis

First, I analyzed our performance with our data analyst, did a benchmark about our main competitor and a user testing. This test allows defining improvements we can made. The benchmark help me to see the choices that our competitors made, good pratices, interesting features. For example, I noticed that a lot of them move toward a design with more spaces, marges, bigger images to propose a selected content to the user.

User put at ease and methodology explanation, he will be able to share his thinking, reactions, feelings

The test start with a free exploration of the website and then with goals or situations

Feedbacks from the user, his questions, point of view

Prototyping

Design of the wireframes, to test different solutions in workshops with other teams (sales, editorial, technical, etc...). We collected their opinions, propositions, feasibility...

User interface

Conception of the graphic mockup : I choose a refined design (less colors whiter, modern, to highlight the brand (we needed to improve the notoriety) and the content. After validation, I wrote the specifications to the developpers, made the feedbacks during the integration, and a survey to the user after the release. It enable to validate our choice or improve the website if needed.

 

 

The site was build in Json to be modular instantly according to the demands coming from the editorial or the interessant news for the user.

Graphical brief writing

Then I wrote the digital guidelines, made templates, create new icons etc..
