LIVING

New charter

The project type : professional
My role : Artistic director & UI designer
Duration : 3 months
Date : April to June 2019


The verticale Living of euronews needed a redesign to match the new positioning :

- About sustainability, conscious lifestyle

- Inform with objectivity, quality and relevance, be « All Views », Serious and trustworthy (with light tone)

- Positive

- Trendy and stylish to attract luxury sponsors


Our audience :

The core target is young, concerned by ecological issues, digital first, affluent and urban with purchasing power, like leisure, travelling. Sustainability will attract a more female audience but we are utlimately aiming at attracting both genders.
