KNOW ME

Mobile design

The project type : school
My role : Designer, artistic director & ergonomist
Team : Mathieu Martin , developer
Duration : 5 months
Date : 2014

Know Me is a mobile application about augmented knowledges for couples
The product is promoted by an interactive installation with a kinect.
Then, the couple log into the mobile application with their Facebook accounts. It is important because we will reuse and reinterpret their personnals informations for this question-and-answer game which takes place in three stages. This app was made to expose the overexposure of our private life on internet.
