BIKE REPAIR

Mobile application

The project type : professional during UX design course
My role : UI & UX designer
Duration : 2 days
Date : 26 & 27 May

During a UX design training, I build a mobile application to guide the users toward a parking and access a local to repair their bike.

Throughout this training, we made ideation and conception workshops to build this app. I learned a lot about design thinking and user centric conception. We made Personas, user experience map, the wireframes and mockups on Figma.
