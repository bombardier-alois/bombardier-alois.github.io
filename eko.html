EKO

Web design

The project type : school
My role : Designer, project manager, artistic director & ergonomist
Team : 16 peoples : 8 graphic designer and 8 developers Gobelins in 4 teams : product, site, mobile, social networks
Duration : 1 week
Date : 2014

We imagined a fictional object : Eko. Eko is a bridge between your feelings and your home devices. The product team designed it in 3D, created the logo and defined the graphic charter. The mobile team created the mobile application sold with Eko. The social networks team drived the buzz on internet. I was the project manager and artistic director for the website team. My mission was to imagine the interface of the Eko website, to report back the progress to the others teams every mornings and guide developers throught the development of the website.
